Broccoli Bowl

Tender beef slices and crunchy Chinese broccoli or kale are stir-fried in a savory ginger sauce. Easy to make, pretty healthy, and everybody loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Broccoli
  • 1 onion
  • 1 ginger root

Steps

  1. Combine 1/4 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules, 1/4 teaspoon sugar, and ginger juice in a bowl.
  2. Mix 2 tablespoons water with cornstarch in a small bowl. Add to bowl with ginger juice mixture; stir.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok over medium-high heat; cook and stir ginger until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add broccoli; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil in the wok.
  5. Add ginger juice mixture and broccoli. Cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
