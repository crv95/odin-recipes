Steps

  1. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes; drain.
  2. Add the onion, a cup of cheese, sour cream, and dried herbs.
  3. Melt butter in a large pot over medium-low heat. Slowly add flour to butter, whisking constantly.
  4. Stir drained macaroni into the cheese sauce to coat.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the sauce is bubbling, 35 to 45 minutes.
